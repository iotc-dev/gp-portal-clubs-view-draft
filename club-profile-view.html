Organisation Overview

About the OrganisationRyde Saints Junior Football Club was established in 1985 and has been serving the Ryde community for nearly 40 years. We provide Australian Rules Football programs for children aged 5-17, with a focus on skill development, teamwork, and community engagement. Our club has grown from 30 members to over 240 registered players across multiple age groups.
Purpose / MissionTo provide a safe, inclusive, and supportive environment for young people to learn and play Australian Rules Football while developing life skills, building friendships, and fostering community connections.
Key Issues / Priorities1. Upgrade aging clubroom facilities to meet modern safety standards.
2. Purchase new training equipment including defibrillators.
3. Expand girls' football program.
4. Improve spectator amenities including shade structures and seating.
Sports / ActivitiesAustralian Rules Football
League AffiliationAFL NSW/ACT, Sydney Junior Football League
